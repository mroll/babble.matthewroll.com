020 - You Still Have to Play Finite Games

Even if you are an inifinite game player and fully grasp the continuum of chained finite games that comprise your domain, you still have to play finite games with finite game players. Maybe you could build like a commune of infinite game players and not have to encounter any finite game players, but then you’d be cutting yourself off from much of the world and it’s not worth it. No, you have to play finite games with finite game players.
