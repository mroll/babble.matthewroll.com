003 - Coffee

Coffee went from being something I knew about but had no experience with, to a daily ritual and something I have a real love for, in like the span of a couple months. It was freshman year of college, and the dining hall had free coffee, and I would take it with some cream and sugar. My friend Joe and I would kind of bond over coffee, I think he was already a coffee drinker and the reason I took an interest to it might have been a way to have something we could share and talk about.
